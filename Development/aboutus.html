Our History

NLB Studios is an industry of No Longer Bound, a 12-month, residential regeneration center for men with drug and alcohol addiction. For 25 years, No Longer Bound has saved over 1,200 men from their deadly addictions, returning regenerated men to their families and society and a chance to lead productive, meaningful lives.

The four industries of No Longer Bound provide revenue for the ministry and allow us to keep intake fees low for men seeking help to overcome addiction. No Longer Bound industries also provide valuable vocational training for residents and a productive way for men to spend their days--In addition to interactive classroom work and group counseling received at No Longer Bound.

Men working on the NLB Studios team gain customer service experience, learn graphic and website design, develop scheduling and inventory expertise, plus other valuable skills. When you visit NLB Studios, you'll see our residents and interns working in all areas of the operation. We hope you'll take a moment to offer a word of encouragement and get to know some of the outstanding residents at No Longer Bound.

No Longer Bound is a faith based, independent 501(c)(3) non-profit charity.
